Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Is there a minimum order?

MOQ is one (1) hide

Are custom orders possible?

Custom orders are always possible

What is the lead time for a CFA?

Cuttings for approval typically take 1 day – 2 weeks depending on the article/color. CFAs are highly recommended for all leathers due to dye lot variations.

What is the lead time for shipping in-stock leather?

In stock leather typically ships between 1 day – 2 weeks depending on the article/color.

What is your return policy?

You have 30 days to dispute and/or return hides. We will not accept hides that have been cut or treated.

What are your account terms?

We must receive advanced full invoice payment prior to shipment.
